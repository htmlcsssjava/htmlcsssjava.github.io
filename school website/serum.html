The serum VST

my real website | image line website | VST review |

The serum VST is a very useful and versitile VST. It can be used to make your own waveforms and add effects to them. It's kind of like a synth but inside of a DAW. It has various presets which can be added to the song, but don't be intimidated by the knobs!
